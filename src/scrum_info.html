Scrum

Scrum is an agile approach at project management.
It iterates through so called sprints, in which you discuss what tasks need to be done, and then work through those tasks in whichever order you want.

Its advantages are:
- High Flexibility
- High Transparency
- High amounts of self-organization

But of course there are some disadvantages:
- High communication demand
- More difficult to handle with multiple development teams
- No complete overview across the project
