Spiral-Model

The Spiral-Model is a repeating process.
Cycles can easily be skipped if the need arises.
Its more flexible approach allows for it tto be used in larger projects.

Its advantages are:
- Higher flexibility in response to changing requirements
- Each cycle can be planned independent from each other
- Allows regular checkups on current progress
- Integrated Quality-Management

Its disadvantages are:
- Not easily parallelized
- Risk-Management is quite difficult
- High management component
