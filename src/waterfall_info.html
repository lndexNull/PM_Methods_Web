Waterfall

The waterfall model is a linear procedure model.
It follows a phases plan and is one of the classic methods used in project-management.

Its advantages are:
- All phases can be planned beforehand
- Phases are strictly seperated
- Time and Cost is more easily calculated
- Documented throughout the runtime

But of course there are some disadvantages:
- Little play for adjustments due to change in requirements
- Client is not part of the development process
- Majority of complexity will be at the start
- Mistakes may only become visible at the end of the project
