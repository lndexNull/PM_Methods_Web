V-Model

The V-Model is a linear model with the unique attribute of mirroring a test-phase for every development-phase.
All test will be specified during the development-phase.
This system makes teh v-model excellent for software-development

Its advantages are:
- Better communication between all parties
- Minimized risks and higher quality-controll
- Works well for large and very large projects

Its disadvantages are:
- Higher complexity and scope
- The flowing nature of the phases makes it hard to distinguish between them
- Adds too much to the scope for small project
